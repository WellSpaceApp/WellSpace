🔒 Privacy Policy

What we collect: Your name, email, mood check-ins, goals, and wellness data.

What teachers can see: Only data you explicitly choose to share. Your private journal, private moods, and private wellness data are never visible to anyone else.

What teachers cannot see: Your password, private journal entries, private mood logs, or any wellness data marked private.

Class codes: Only your teacher can create class codes. You cannot add yourself to a class without a valid teacher-issued code.

Data storage: All data is stored locally on this device (localStorage). Nothing is sent to any server.

Your rights: You can delete your data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.
